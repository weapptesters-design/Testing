Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your 14-day test.

Do you use real Android devices?
Yes. Our testing network consists entirely of real humans using physical Android devices. We strictly avoid bots or emulators to ensure your app meets Play Console requirements authentically.
Do you provide screenshot proof?
Screenshot proof reports are automatically included in our Premium plans. For Standard plans, they can be requested as an optional add-on during checkout.
Can you help with production questions?
Absolutely. After the 14-day testing period, developers must answer specific questions in the Play Console to apply for production access. We provide detailed guidance on how to accurately describe the testing process.
