Refund Policy

Last updated: October 2024

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are applicable only if your app is officially rejected for Google Play Production Access after completing the full 14-day closed testing cycle with our team.

Official Google rejection confirmation is mandatory to process any refund request.

Required Documentation

  • Proof of purchase
  • Official Google rejection email
  • Confirmation of 14-day testing completion
  • Requests must be submitted within 6 days of rejection.

Resolution Options

If eligible, you may choose one of the following resolution options:

  • Full refund of the service amount
  • One free re-test period with our total 25+ testers

Processing Timeline

  • Review within 1–3 business days
  • Approved refunds processed within 1–5 business days

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be granted under the following circumstances:

  • Policy violations unrelated to testing (e.g., copyright infringement, malware, content violations)
  • Incomplete or unstable builds provided to testers
  • Failure to follow setup instructions
  • Fraudulent documentation
